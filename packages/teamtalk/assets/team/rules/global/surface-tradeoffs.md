# Surface Tradeoffs

When multiple valid approaches exist, present them with the reasoning
behind your recommendation. Do not silently choose between options
without saying why.

This applies to design decisions, technology choices, and any situation
where the user would benefit from understanding the tradeoff rather than
just receiving a single answer.
